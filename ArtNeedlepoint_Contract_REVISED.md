## 1. SERVICES PROVIDED

Reputation Heal will build and deploy a custom AI Employee Team for The Art Needlepoint Company, configured specifically for their business operations. The AI team consists of five specialized agents and supporting automation workflows, as described below.

### Your AI Employee Team

**ATLAS — Revenue Recovery Agent**
Monitors WooCommerce for cart abandonments and failed payments. Executes a 3-touch email recovery sequence (1 hour, 24 hours, 72 hours) with smart incentives based on cart contents. Checks WooCommerce in real time — if an order is completed at any point, the sequence stops automatically.

**IRIS — Review Intelligence Agent**
Scores every incoming customer email using AI sentiment analysis (scale of 1–10). Happy customers (score 8+) are queued for a personalized review request. Customers with concerns (score below 8) are routed directly and privately to Doreen — they are never sent a public review request.

**ECHO — Email Automation Agent**
Generates personalized review request emails using the customer's own words from their testimonials. Every draft is submitted to Doreen for review and approval before any email is sent to a customer. Nothing leaves without Doreen's explicit sign-off.

**SCOUT — SEO Watchdog Agent**
Monitors Google Search Console daily for ranking changes, index coverage shifts, and new spam page injections. Flags any new pages with the hacker author signature ("andevtemp") immediately as a security alert.

**HERALD — Content Publisher Agent**
Generates SEO-optimized category page content, title tags, and meta descriptions for Art Needlepoint's product catalog. All content is submitted to Doreen for review and approval before publication.

### Operations Command Center (Hermes Dashboard)
A private web portal provided to The Art Needlepoint Company showing:
- Daily revenue, SEO health, reviews, and recovery statistics
- All pending Doreen approvals (emails and content drafts)
- Full agent activity log and workflow status
- Cart abandonment tracking and recovery outcomes

## 9. LIABILITY & PERFORMANCE COMMITMENTS

**9.1 Company Liability Cap — Plain English Explanation**
This clause means: if something goes wrong and The Art Needlepoint Company suffers a direct loss caused by Reputation Heal's actions, the maximum amount Reputation Heal is financially responsible for is equal to whatever fees The Art Needlepoint Company has paid in the three (3) months before the issue arose. This protects both parties from unlimited open-ended claims while ensuring Reputation Heal has real financial accountability for its work. It does not limit Reputation Heal's obligation to fix problems — that obligation is unlimited (see 9.4).

---

Reputation Heal is engaged for its expertise and commits to the following measurable performance targets. These are not vague promises — they are specific, trackable milestones visible on the Hermes portal.

**Minimum Performance Thresholds** — these are the floor. If we fall below these, Section 9.3 is triggered automatically:

| "Needlepoint kits" keyword | Move from position ~14 to position 10 or better | Within 90 days | Google Search Console |

| Cart recovery emails | System operational and sending within 48 hours of launch | Week 1 | Hermes Dashboard |

**9.3 No Results / Underperformance Scenario**

If The Art Needlepoint Company believes Reputation Heal has failed to meet the Minimum Performance Thresholds in Section 9.2:

1. The Art Needlepoint Company raises the concern in writing (email is sufficient)
2. Reputation Heal responds within **5 business days** with a written remediation plan including specific corrective actions and a revised timeline
3. Both Parties have **14 days** to agree on the remediation plan
4. If agreement cannot be reached, The Art Needlepoint Company may terminate this Agreement with **30 days written notice** and will owe no further fees beyond the current billing period
5. Any fees paid for a period in which minimum thresholds were clearly and demonstrably not met will be reviewed in good faith for a partial refund

---

**9.4 System Issues & Harm Correction**

In the event that any AI system, workflow, automation, or content deployed under this Agreement causes harm to The Art Needlepoint Company's business, website, customer relationships, or reputation:

- Reputation Heal will take **immediate corrective action at no additional cost**
- Reputation Heal will communicate the issue and fix plan to Doreen Finkel within **24 hours** of becoming aware of it
- Reputation Heal will work in good faith to remediate any damage caused, including rebuilding or replacing any affected systems
- This obligation to correct and remediate is **not capped** by Section 9.1

**9.5 Limitation on Indirect Losses**
Neither Party shall be liable to the other for indirect, incidental, or consequential losses that are not directly and proximately caused by a proven breach of this Agreement. This clause does not limit either Party's responsibility for direct, demonstrable harm.
