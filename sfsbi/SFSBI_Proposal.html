02 / SECURITY AUDIT
Before We Build — Something We Found

Your patient email is unprotected

During our pre-build audit of sfsbi.com, we found two critical email security gaps that leave your practice exposed to spoofing attacks.

CRITICAL: No DMARC Record

Right now, anyone can send an email that appears to come from sheena@sfsbi.com or info@sfsbi.com — and the receiving inbox has no way to know it's fake. For a medical practice where patients share health information via email, this is a HIPAA-adjacent exposure.

HIGH: SPF Record Misconfigured

Your email runs through Google Workspace, but your SPF record doesn't include Google's servers. Your own legitimate emails may be failing authentication — landing in spam or being rejected entirely.

We'll Fix This — Included at No Extra Cost

Two DNS record changes. Takes 30 minutes. We'll add DMARC, fix the SPF, and set up the bare domain redirect (sfsbi.com currently shows an error instead of your website). This is done as part of our onboarding.
