04

Automation Workflows

⚡ WORKFLOW 1

Post-Call Handler

Triggers: Every time a call ends
  • 1
    Retell webhook fires with call data
  • 2
    Extract 15 fields from payload
  • 3
    Save to Supabase → dashboard updates in real-time
  • 4
    If urgent → SMS alert to Sheena immediately
🚨 WORKFLOW 2

Urgent Escalation

Triggers: urgency_flag = "urgent" or "emergency"
  • 1
    Detects urgent or emergency flag
  • 2
    Sends immediate SMS to Sheena's phone
  • 3
    Includes caller name, phone, and reason
📧 WORKFLOW 3

Weekly Report

Triggers: Every Monday at 9:00 AM
  • 1
    Pull last 7 days of calls from Supabase
  • 2
    Generate summary: totals, leads, top services
  • 3
    Email full report to Sheena
